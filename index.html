I am currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science at DePaul University, focusing on Human Computer Interaction (HCI). I am interested in applying HCI methods and techniques to understand diverse users of computer systems.

In my PhD study, I am especially focused on (1) games user research (GUR), (2) game design research, and (3) accessibility of computer systems, including video games. The main project I am currently working on is aimed at supporting the use and creation of games for brain injury rehabilitation.
