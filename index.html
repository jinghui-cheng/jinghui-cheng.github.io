Assistant Professor
Department of Computer Engineering,
Polytechnique Montréal

My research combines the field of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) with Software Engineering. My approach is fundamentally human-centered, with an emphasis on exploring technologies that support practitioners who have domain-specific expertise but special information needs, including software engineers, game designers, and medical experts.

I am looking for motivated master or PhD students to join my research team. Please email me if interested.
